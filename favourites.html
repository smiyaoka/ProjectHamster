The site admins share some of their favourite anime. Post a review in the forums if you agree/disagree with our choices!

Nick

Favorites

1. Cowboy Bebop
Bounty hunters in space search for criminals.
Director: Shinichirō Watanabe
Cast: Kōichi Yamadera, Megumi Hayashibara, Unshō Ishizuka
Program creators: Sunrise, Bandai Visual
Genres: Neo-noir, Space Western

2. Steins;Gate
Steins;Gate follows a self-proclaimed mad scientist named Rintarō Okabe who, along with his friends, manage to invent a microwave that can send text messages into the past, allowing him to travel between alternate worlds.
Director: Hiroshi Hamasaki
Studio: White Fow

3. Noragami
A minor god seeking to gain widespread worship teams up with a human girl he saved to gain fame, recognition and at least one shrine.
Production studio: Bones

4. Gargantia on the Verdurous Planet
In the future, Ledo, a young soldier of the Galactic Alliance of Humankind, finds himself on Earth.
Director: Kazuya Murata
Program creators: Production IG, Bandai Visual
Genres: Mecha Anime

5. Shin Chan
Series follows the adventures of the five-year-old Shinnosuke "Shin" Nohara and his parents, baby sister, dog, neighbours, and friends and is set in Kasukabe, Saitama Prefecture.
Production Studio: Shin-Ei Animation
Genres: Black Comedy, Slice of life, Slapstick
